It is an automated screen, not a recommendation

The Buffett Analysis is generated automatically by software on a scheduled basis. It applies fixed, mechanical filters to publicly available financial data. It does not involve human judgment, individualized analysis, or any evaluation of your personal financial situation, goals, or risk tolerance.

  • a solicitation or offer to buy or sell any security; or
  • a prediction or guarantee of future performance.

The criteria are mechanical and limited

The screen relies on historical and publicly reported data, which may be delayed, incomplete, or inaccurate. A stock meeting these criteria has only satisfied a set of numeric filters — it has not been evaluated for current events, valuation relative to price, management quality, competitive position, or any forward-looking factor. Stocks that do not appear may nonetheless be appropriate investments, and stocks that do appear may be poor investments.
