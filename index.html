About

1010Grow helps you pause before a purchase and put that money toward investing instead. Before you spend $20 on an impulse buy, the app shows you what that amount could be worth invested over time — then helps you act on it, with a simulated portfolio to practice or your own connected brokerage when you're ready.

How the InvestWise flow works

  1. Enter what you were about to spend money on.
  2. See the opportunity cost — what that amount could grow to if invested instead.
  3. Note your mood and tie the choice to your goals.
  4. Choose a stock or ETF yourself, optionally referencing the monthly Buffett screen.
  5. Place a paper trade to practice, or a live trade through your own connected brokerage.

You choose every stock and confirm every trade. 1010Grow does not recommend securities or trade on your behalf.

Features

Spend-to-invest nudge

Turn a would-be purchase into an investing decision and see its long-term opportunity cost.

Paper trading

Practice investing with a virtual portfolio — free, with no real money at risk.

Buffett screen

A monthly, rules-based screen that surfaces stocks meeting value-investing criteria — high return on equity, low P/E, low debt. Informational only; you decide what to do with it.

Portfolio tracking

Follow your paper and live portfolios in one place.

Connect your brokerage (Premium)

Premium subscribers can link their own Alpaca or Charles Schwab account to place self-directed trades.
